Overview:

Eros is found on Pluto. The purpose of Eros is to cure sickle cell anemia. Sickle cell anemia is one of a group of inherited disorders known as sickle cell disease. It affects the shape of red blood cells, which carry oxygen to all parts of the body. 

Red blood cells are usually round and flexible, so they move easily through blood vessels. In sickle cell anemia, some red blood cells are shaped like sickles or crescent moons. These sickle cells also become rigid and sticky, which can slow or block blood flow.  

The patient can either drink the flower or swallow it in pill form. This flower, by the name of Eros, changes the shape of the red blood cells after 4 weeks of daily use. After the initial 4 weeks the user will need to continue once daily pills or 8oz of pre-measured herbal tea for 2 consecutive years. After 2 years the patient will be completely cured The plant also increases the life span of red blood cells, decreasing the anemia symptom.
